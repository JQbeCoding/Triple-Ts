# **Triple T's Ideas**

## **Brand Vibe**

- Taylor says she wants to have a cozy and cute vibe for her business.
- Her ideal customer is women, specifically women who love cookies!
- She likes the key term "Boutique," and the branding will be artisanal like her cookies. 
- She wants to stray away from corporate looks and be truly unique within the space.
- Her marketing is very digital, so an easy-to-use platform is ideal.
- Her packaging will also be neat and cute, so the visual should be sharp!

## **Customer Vision**
**This is the path from discovery to order** 
- They search for cookies.
- Views Triple T's marketing via social media and potentially search engines.
- Customer visits Taylor's business and is stunned by the visual presence and fair pricing.
- They then place an order
- Customer is satisfied, and happily follows to stay informed.
**Alternatively**
- Customers anticipate the weekend for cookies.
- Customer gets a notification when the cookies are ready for pre-order.
- Customer orders cookies.

## **Features**
- Taylor prefers to keep it strictly business, so nothing like a loyalty program or discounts, although she is open to the idea in the future.
- No crazy animations, just a clean site. Preferably a single-page app.
